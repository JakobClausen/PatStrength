Crossfit Programming

Monday

Strength Conditioning

EMOM 6 Rounds

3-5 RMU (Ring Muscle up)
3 power snatches


Description:
Do 3-5 RMU the first minute, 3 power snatches the second then 3-5 RMU the third minute and so on.

Loading should be heavy but with good form.


Strength

Back Squat

3x6 @ 70-80% of 1rm


Description:
Should be heavy but also with good form. Try to get used to having heavy weight on your back.


Metcon

21-15-9-6
Cal Row
Devils Press
50 DU (Double unders) between sets


Description:
It´s all about the Devils Presses in this wod. Try to recover on the rower and DU and push the pace on the Devils Presses.
Focus on your breath.

Tuseday

Olympic lifting

Every 90 seconds for 6 rounds

2 Power Cleans


Description:
The goal is to go heavy but with good form.

Warm up to a heavy double with good form and keep that weight for the 6 rounds. Only add weight if form is perfect.


Clean Pull

3x5 @ 75-85% of c&j (clean and jerk) 1rm(one rep max)


Description:
Should be heavy but also with good form. Try to focus on using your legs to explode up. Keep your whole foot on the ground during the lift to generate maximum power.


Metcon

3 min amrap

6 STOH (Shoulder To Overhead) @ 50kg/35kg
6 TTB (Toes To Bar)


Rest 3 min


3 min amrap

30 DU (Double Unders)
8/6 cal assault bike


Rest 3 min


3 min amrap

6 double KB swing @24kg/16kg
6 burpee box jump


Description:
Go har during the three minutes then try to get your heartrate down as much as you can during the three minute rest.
Focus on your breath.

Wednesday

Strength Conditioning

Emom for 12 rounds

5 Dead lift 70-75%

5 Strict HSPU


Description:
Find your 75% of 1 RM in your deadlift. Add weight if needed.

If 5 strict HSPU is too easy, add weights and do deficit.


Strength

Back Squat

5x3 85% of 1 RM

Description:
You can add weight after every set but focus on good form.


Bodybuilding

3 round for time

5 weighted pull ups

5 weighted dips

15 weighted GHD sit ups


Description:
Find a weight where you can perform maximum 5 strict pull ups/dips.

The last rep should be close to failure. Same goes with GHD sit ups.

Rest Day

Friday

Olympic Lifting

A

1 thruster + push press + power jerk + split jerk
Find your heaviest complex of the day.


Description:
Start with the bar on the ground and try to go ”touch and go” on each rep.

If you drop the bar, you have to start over from thruster.


B

Split jerk

4x3 @ a heavy and challenging weight.


Description:
Focus on good form and being exlposive.

If struggling with balance in the catch position, do a 2-3 second paus in the catch.


Metcon

For time
10 back squats 130 KG
40 chest to bar pull ups
20 back squats 110 KG

20 bar muscle ups
30 back squats 90 KG
10 ring muscle ups


Description:
Try to do as big sets as possible but never fail a rep. Be smart.
Long and heavy workout focusing on good form and being able to sting as many pullups/C2B/Muscle up when breathing heavily.
Focus on your breath.
